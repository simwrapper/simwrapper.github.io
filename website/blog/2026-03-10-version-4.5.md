Announcing the release of SimWrapper 4.5, available now on pip, at **https://simwrapper.app**, and on [GitHub](https://github.com/simwrapper/simwrapper).

- In addition to bugfixes,  the big new feature with this release is support for [AequilibraE networks](/docs/aequilibrae-map), which can now be displayed using the `aequilibrae` view plugin. We're excited to welcome AequilibraE users into the SimWrapper family :-)

- The Sankey chart plugin also learned some new sort/unsort capabilities, useful if you want the chart to always display options in the same order instead of in alphabetical order (both are useful in different contexts).

The full changelog is at [SimWrapper Changelog](/docs/changelog)

Feedback or questions? Please start a chat or follow along on the [SimWrapper discussion board](https://github.com/orgs/simwrapper/discussions) if you have suggestions, comments, cheers & jeers!

We are working on lots of other new features and continue to sharpen SimWrapper based on your feedback! Thanks for your support 🙏
